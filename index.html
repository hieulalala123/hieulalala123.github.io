§ Focus

Machine Learning

Models that predict and classify accurately, even with messy or imbalanced data. I lean on gradient boosting, ensembles, and feature engineering, backed by rigorous evaluation so that models which look good in testing actually hold up in production.

Econometrics

The statistical backbone for finance. Proficient in time-series, panel data, and logistic/linear regressions, along with the precise diagnostics that separate true financial signals from random noise.

Causal Inference

Moving beyond simple correlation to true causation. I utilize Diff-in-Diff, propensity score matching, uplift modeling, and debiased ML—the exact tools needed for data-driven decision making, not just forecasting.

Computational Statistics

The machinery for complex estimation. Proficient in resampling methods, Bootstrapping, and Monte Carlo simulations to stress-test financial models, estimate non-linear parameters, and construct robust confidence intervals.
